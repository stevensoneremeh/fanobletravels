GREECE

Discover Greece: A Biblical Tour

FOLLOW FANOBLE TRAVELS AND TOURS ON A REGIOUS ADVENTURE TO GREECE

Embark on a profound Christian pilgrimage through Greece, where ancient spirituality and awe-inspiring landscapes converge to create an unforgettable journey of faith. Begin in Athens, tracing the footsteps of Apostle Paul at the Areopagus and exploring early Christian sites amidst the city's ancient marvels. Journey to Delphi to reflect on the transition from paganism to Christianity, then immerse yourself in the tranquility of Meteora's monasteries, where Orthodox monks seek spiritual enlightenment amidst towering rock formations. Continue your pilgrimage to Mount Athos, a haven of prayer and contemplation, and explore the sacred island of Patmos, where St. John received divine revelations, offering a glimpse into the early Christian era. Join us on this spiritual odyssey through Greece, where each site holds echoes of saints and apostles, guiding you on a journey of profound faith and discovery.
